### Ingredients

- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
- 1/2 (1 stick) cup softened unsalted butter
- 3/4 cup brown sugar
- 2 large eggs
- 1 small container plain yogurt or sour cream
- 2 cups mashed ripe bananas (about 5)
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 3/4 cup chopped walnuts

### Instructions

Preheat oven to 350°F and grease a 9×5-inch loaf pan with butter.

In a bowl, whisk together flour, baking soda, salt, and cinnamon.

Cream butter and sugar until smooth with a solid spatula. Beat in eggs one at a time, then mix in yogurt, 4 bananas, and vanilla. Save a banana for the top.

Gradually mix dry ingredients into the wet mixture until just combined. Fold in nuts if desired.

Pour batter into the loaf pan. Bake for 60–65 minutes, covering loosely with foil halfway through. Check doneness with a toothpick.

Let the bread cool in the pan for 1 hour, then transfer to a rack to cool completely.
